How to apply for pre-claim approval

Apply for pre-claim approval before you begin your SR&ED project.

What is pre-claim approval

Pre-claim approval allows businesses to have their planned work evaluated to ensure it meets SR&ED requirements before beginning their project and incurring significant costs.
Learn more: Optional pre-claim approval

Applying for pre-claim approval is optional. If you want to submit an SR&ED claim without pre-claim approval, go to: How to prepare your SR&ED claim.

Criteria for pre-claim approval

Your planned work must meet all the eligibility requirements for an SR&ED tax incentive. In addition, you must meet all the following criteria for pre-claim approval.

Your business must:

  • Be either a Canadian-controlled private corporation or other Canadian corporation, or a partnership
  • Have a gross business income of less than $25M
  • Be in good standing with the CRA

The project must:

  • Not have already been claimed in a previous tax year
  • Not involve issues that may be currently under litigation

When to apply

If you apply for pre-claim approval before you begin your planned work, you can ensure it meets SR&ED requirements before you incur any significant costs. If you have already begun your work, you can still apply for pre-claim approval.

How to apply

Follow the steps below to apply for pre-claim approval for SR&ED tax incentives.

  1. Request a case number

    Confirm you meet the criteria and get the case number you need to submit your application

  2. Complete your application

    Answer questions about your project, gather and submit your supporting documents

  3. Submit your application

    Upload your application in My Business Account in your CRA account
